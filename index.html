What is Triage?

Triage is an on-demand application that authentically simulates a disaster, the purpose of which is to train first responders to correctly act in a real situation.

Why did we do it?

We saw the need to bring emergency response training to the 21st century! We aim to bring the highest quality of education possible so we can train up and coming first responders!
