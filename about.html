About

“Jumpstart Triage” is an on-demand application that authentically simulates a disaster. It trains first responders to correctly act in a real situation! We've also incorporated the "Jumpstart" Triage algorythm for response.

Currently our simulation includes:

  • A full 3D environment.
  • Multiple victims.
  • Ability to assess.
  • Use different types of equipment on victims.
  • Tagging victims with color.

However we've only just begun!

In the future, we will include:

  • Multiplayer.
  • More Scenarios.
  • More Environments.
  • Different Roles.
  • Interchangeable Parameters .

It is our mission to make a simulation that will encompass all the elements of responding to a disaster. Allowing us to improve the training of future emergency responders.
